Pitambari

Logo Designing

Pitambari

Logo Design

About Work

Pitambari is an emerging brand of south indian dress materials and kurtis based in Ahmedabad, India. They create their very own designs and giving cutting edge competitions to their rivals all around India.

The team wanted to logo which can connect with the hindu mythology and modern 21st century design. So my challenge was to first understand the meaning of the word Pitambari and need to find a way to connect it with the 21st century. Pitambari is word derived from word Pitambar which was worn by lord shree krishana and another meaning is related with color that is yellowish orange. So I need to take care of all the things while creating a logo. I've sketched almost 20 to 25 loges and finaly they have selected one.

I've merged two different fonts to get both the major things that is 21st century and hindu mythology. Color was selected yellowish orange as major and Ocimum tenuiflorum used with modern design style and connect with hindu mythology. For letter head, They have specified one character that is center of page which is taken from their parent brand and that is their icon.
